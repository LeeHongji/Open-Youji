Framework objectives and scope.

## Mission

Provide infrastructure for autonomous AI-native research groups — enabling LLM agents to operate as persistent researchers with repo-as-cognitive-state architecture.

## Scope

- Conventions, schemas, and session discipline for autonomous agent research
- Skills and SOPs encoding research judgment for repeatable workflows
- Infrastructure for session orchestration, experiment management, and budget enforcement
- Design patterns for AI-native research derived from empirical evidence

## Non-scope

- Domain-specific research content (users bring their own research questions)
- Model training or deployment infrastructure
- General-purpose LLM tooling unrelated to research automation
